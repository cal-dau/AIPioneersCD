Ashish Vaswani

Co-Inventor of Transformers

Early Life & Career

  • Born in India.
  • Studied at IIT Kanpur, then Ph.D. at USC.
  • Joined Google Brain as a researcher in NLP.

Transformers

In 2017, Vaswani co-authored the landmark paper *“Attention is All You Need”*, introducing the Transformer architecture.

  • Revolutionized natural language processing.
  • Enabled GPT, BERT, and modern large language models.
  • Replaced recurrent models with attention mechanisms.

Enduring Legacy

Vaswani’s work reshaped AI, enabling breakthroughs in language, vision, and multimodal models that define today’s AI era.
