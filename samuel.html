Arthur Samuel

Pioneer of Machine Learning

Early Life & Career

  • Born in 1901 in Emporia, Kansas.
  • Studied electrical engineering at MIT.
  • Worked at Bell Labs and later IBM.

Machine Learning Work

Samuel developed one of the earliest self-learning programs: a checkers-playing program at IBM in the 1950s.

  • First to use the term "machine learning."
  • Program improved its strategy the more it played.
