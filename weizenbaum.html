Joseph Weizenbaum

Creator of ELIZA, Critic of AI

Early Life

  • Born in Berlin, Germany, in 1923.
  • Escaped Nazi Germany, emigrated to the U.S. in 1936.
  • Studied mathematics at Wayne State University.

ELIZA Program

In 1966, Weizenbaum created ELIZA, a natural language processing program that simulated a psychotherapist.

  • One of the first chatbot-style programs.
  • Exposed how easily people anthropomorphize machines.
