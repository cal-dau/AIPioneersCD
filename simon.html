Herbert Simon

Polymath & AI Pioneer

Early Life

  • Born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, in 1916.
  • Studied political science at the University of Chicago.
  • Turned toward decision-making and artificial intelligence research.

Contributions

With Allen Newell, co-developed the Logic Theorist and General Problem Solver.

  • Pioneered symbolic AI.
  • Researched human problem-solving processes.
  • Nobel Prize in Economics (1978) for decision-making theories.

Enduring Legacy

Herbert Simon bridged AI, economics, psychology, and management science. His work on bounded rationality and AI shaped multiple fields.
